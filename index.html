About me

With nearly 10 years of experience, I am a skilled eyelash extension specialist dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty. Offering a range of services, including classic, volume, and hybrid lash extensions, I carefully select high-quality lashes to create stunning, long-lasting results. I prioritize your satisfaction, providing personalized consultations and expert advice to ensure the perfect lash look for you. Trust me to deliver professional and reliable services that leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Classic eylashes

Classic lash extensions involve applying a single lash extension to each natural lash. This technique provides a natural and subtle enhancement, adding length and definition to your lashes. It is perfect for those who desire a more understated look while still achieving noticeable results.

Volume eyelashes

Volume lash extensions involve the application of multiple ultra-fine lash extensions to each natural lash, creating a full and glamorous look. This technique allows for customized levels of volume, ranging from a soft and natural appearance to a dramatic and bold effect. Volume lashes are ideal for those seeking a more intense and voluminous look.

Hybrid eyelashes

Hybrid lash extensions combine both classic and volume techniques. By strategically placing a mix of individual lash extensions and handmade volume fans, this style offers a beautiful blend of natural and fullness. It creates a textured, multidimensional effect, adding volume and density to your lashes while maintaining a soft and fluttery appearance.
